    Bahubali Thali

    The Bahubali Thali at SwadGhar is an extravagant and regal dining experience.

    It features an opulent spread of traditional Indian dishes, including a lavish assortment of curries, rice, bread, and delectable desserts. This thali is fit for a king, offering a grand and indulgent taste of India's culinary heritage.

    7 Sisters of India Thali

    SwadGhar's "7 Sisters of India Thali" is a unique culinary experience that celebrates the northeastern states of India.

    This special thali showcases a delightful array of vegetarian dishes from the seven sisters, offering a taste of the region's diverse and authentic flavors in one delicious meal.

    South Indian Thali

    The South Indian Thali at SwadGhar is a delightful feast that includes 14 diverse curry and side dishes, accompanied by a variety of bread options, and a crispy papad.

    This culinary journey allows you to experience the rich and vibrant flavors of South India in a single, satisfying meal.

    Satvik Thali

    SwadGhar's "Satvik Thali" is a wholesome and pure vegetarian feast that adheres to the principles of Satvik cuisine.

    It features a delectable assortment of dishes made with simple, fresh ingredients, devoid of garlic and onions, providing a serene and balanced culinary experience.

    All States Thali

    SwadGhar's "All States Thali" is a culinary journey that captures the diverse and vibrant flavors of India.

    This special thali showcases a selection of signature dishes from various Indian states, offering a unique and delicious way to experience the country's rich and varied culinary heritage in a single meal.
